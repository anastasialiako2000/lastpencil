LAST PENCIL

Game Rules

1.The players choose the number of pencils they want to play with.

2.The game needs to know who is going to start first. They play alternately.

3.Each player chooses a number of pencils that she/he wants to take.

4.If the player has no other option rather than take the last pencil, she/he loses, and the other player wins.

The interesting thing about that game is the

Winning strategy

The idea : if there are 2, 3, 4 or 6, 7, 8 and so on, pencils left on the table, the player whose turn is automatically wins.
It happens because a player can take 1, 2, or 3 pencils and leave the other player with only one to take and lose the game.
